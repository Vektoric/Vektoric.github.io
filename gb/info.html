About Galaxy Brain

Info by I.C. Wright

Galaxy Brain is the first comic of all I. C. Wright, hammer, and F.F.. It is first and foremost a learning project!

Galaxy Brain was drawn on Discord Jamspace Whiteboard, Our method was to take turns to draw each panel, until we run out of space, and end the page.
